Effective (re)searching

Brainstorm the topic

Use Mind map as a tool to clarify and understand the key concepts of the topic.

Finde key words

Find the relevant key words in given contents for your topic. It can be used in conjunction with scanning.

Boolean search

A method of combine words and phrases using the words "and", "or" and "not" while searching.

Evaluate your search results

Review your research results and repeat steps 2 and 3 if necessary. Also, see "What are credible, reliable sources?" for better understanding.

Skim and scan

See "Skimming" and "scanning" for more information to decide which items you will use and which you will discard.

Make notes

An effective note should include title, author(s) and datepublished. Always note page numbers against individual notes to save time.

Assistance

Ask your lecturer, ALLSP teamor librarian for assistance.
